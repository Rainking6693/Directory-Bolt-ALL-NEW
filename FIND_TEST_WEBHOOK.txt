
🔍 FINDING 'SEND TEST WEBHOOK' IN STRIPE

📍 Where to Look:

1. Click the '...' (three dots) menu next to 'Edit destination'
   → Look for 'Send test event'

2. Click 'Events' in top navigation bar
   → Look for 'Send test event' button at top right

3. Click 'Edit destination' button
   → Scroll down to find test section

4. Click 'Interactive webhook endpoint builder' under Resources
   → Use this tool to test

⚡ Quickest Way: Use Stripe CLI
   stripe trigger checkout.session.completed

⚠️  Note: Your endpoint is /api/webhooks/stripe
   (This matches your stripe.js file - that's correct!)
